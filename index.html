simplemmobot

What it does:

  • Auto generate enemy and fight on pre-selected tier
  • Auto quest the first one appears at the top
  • Auto step

How to use:

  • unzip rar
  • run simplemmo_bot_web.exe (only)
  • enter id on console press enter
  • enter pw on console press enter
  • let the bot run

TIP: It is possible to open the same account on different tab at the same browser and keep playing the game manually. Just dont step/quest or battle, you can sell your items, chat with people, pray to your god etc.. When you are done just swtich back to the tab which bot working
